Battleship rules:
  • Player 1 = Godzilla, Player 2 = Monkey
  • There are 7 ships hidden on the grid positioned vertically and horizontally.
  • Each ship has a total length of between 2 and 5 consecutive circles.
  • Click on the circles to guess where the ships are located.
  • To sink a ship you have to click all of that ship's circles.
  • The clicked circle and the center message box above the grid will tell you if itâ€™s a hit or miss, and when you have sunk a ship.
  • The player that can sink all their battleships in the fewest attempts wins the game.
